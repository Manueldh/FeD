About Zentry

Our project

Introducing the metagame

Zentry pioneers the Metagame, a Game of Games ecosystem where worlds converge and communities connect, building the foundation for what will soon become the largest virtual economy—the 'Play Economy'—as the era of gaming culture rises.

Our mission

We build, invest and collaborate to craft the world's largest shared adventure.

The Metagame augments social coordination, attention and advertising, propelling Zentry to become the largest distribution network of the new era.

Our architecture

Metagame stack

Zentry is powered by a unique vertically integrated stack – consumer apps, an open IP universe and a blockchain protocol.

Open ip universe

Zentry goes beyond platforms with a content-rich environment. Brimming with stories, quests, characters and assets from collaborative IPs across worlds.

Metagame protocol

The foundation of Zentry, this omnichain protocul fuses a series of modules like Zentry-ID, Social Graph, Proof of Play and more to unify, process and gamify on-chain and off-chain data, as well as Zentry-SDK for developers' and partners' integration.

Game-like experiences

Crafting the real-life MMORPG experience

Zentry brings the MMORPG experience to real life, gamifying the physical and digital — transforming everyday gaming, social, and real-world activities into thrilling adventure.

Technological innovation

Innovation is what we embrace

Zentry merges social, gaming, identity, AI and blockchain together into one cohesive layer. Utilizing its tech elements to foster open collaboration and empower users.

Expansion & growth

Investments and partnershps at scale

Growing our partner network through strategic investment and alliances, which in turn supercharges scaling through our growth flywheel.
